get to know my parents!

RUSLI

The head of the family. A wise and disciplined 55 year old man from Kuala Kurau, Perak. Rusli works as the general manager of the Malaysian outlet of the Japanese company, Dynapac(M) Sdn. Bhd. Additionally acting as the head accountant, it is no surprise that Rusli is a very strict individual and will stop at nothing to accomplish his goals. However, underneath the cold exterior, Rusli is a warm, loving father who always prioritizes the wellbeing of his kids.
Rusli enjoys multiple pastime hobbies including playing golf, playing guitar and other stringed instruments such as the ukulele.

SOFIA

The true head of the family. As the saying goes, behind every successful man is a successful woman. Sofia is a hardworking and dedicated woman. Balancing the struggles of a being a mother of 4 and having a full-time job as a senior electrical engineer at ON Semiconductor, Sofia is the perfect blend of both professional and casual. A great cook, a great mother and a great individual overall, Sofia is the loving mother of this chaotic household.
Additionally, Sofia asides from tending to her many responsibilities, enjoys gardening, reading and jamming to karaoke in her free time.
