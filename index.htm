In the Rusli household,

There lived a wild group of 6 individuals who all were under one roof in a little one-story house in the small city of Seremban, Negeri Sembilan. Although the house may be small and tight, the 4 walls of the house held together the most incredible stories and journey between the entaglement of these 6 individuals in building a happy family

WELCOME TO SEREMBAN CITY!

My family and I have all resided im Seremban, Negeri Sembilan ever since my mother and father married in 1994. My mother, born and raised in Seremban, offered to reside here in Paroi to my father when she bought the spacious piece of land of which our house is currently on. Therefore, all 3 of my siblings and I area all Seremban people at heart! We all went to school at SK Taman Tasik Jaya, which was a primary secondary that was actually just a walk's away from our house. However, we all went separate ways in terms of secondary school as my eldest siblings went to SMK King George V while the younger 2 enrolled in MRSM Alor Gajah in Melaka.
